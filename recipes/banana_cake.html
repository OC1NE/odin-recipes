Banana Cake

Scrumptious & Irresistably Delicious

This banana cake recipe uses freshly mashed bananas as well as buttermilk, resulting in a very moist cake packed with banana flavor! I first came across this recipe on All Recipes. All credits to Cindy Carnes, the original poster of the recipe, as well as her friend who made this cake for her during a visit, After she had delivered her 11th child!

Ingredients


Cake:


  • 1 ½ cups mashed bananas
  • 2 teasponns lemon juice
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 ½ teaspoons baking soda
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • 2 cups white sugar
  • ¾ cup butter
  • 3 large eggs
  • 2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 ½ cups buttermilk

Frosting:


  • ½ cup butter, softened
  • 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 cups confectioners sugar
