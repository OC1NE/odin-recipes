Banana Cake

Irresistably Delicious

This banana cake recipe uses freshly mashed bananas as well as buttermilk, resulting in a very moist cake packed with banana flavor! I first came across this recipe on All Recipes. All credits to Cindy Carnes, the original poster of the recipe, as well as her friend who made this cake for her during a visit, After she had delivered her 11th child!

I made a slight adjustment, with regards to the amount of sugar called for, to the original recipe.

Ingredients

Cake:

  • 1 ½ cups mashed bananas
  • 2 teasponns lemon juice
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 ½ teaspoons baking soda
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • 2 cups white sugar
  • ¾ cup butter
  • 3 large eggs
  • 2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 ½ cups buttermilk

Frosting:

  • ½ cup butter, softened
  • 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 cups confectioners sugar

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 F (175 C). Grease a 9x13-inch pan
  2. Mix mashed bananas with lemon juice in a small bowl.
  3. Mix flour, baking soda, and salt in a medium bowl.
  4. Cream sugar and butter in a large bowl until light and fluffy, 3 to 4 minutes.
  5. Beat in eggs one at a time, then stir in vanilla.
  6. Beat in flour mixture, alternately with the buttermilk. Stir in the banana mixture.
  7. Pour batter into the prepared pan.
  8. Bake in the preheated oven for about 1 hour, or until the cake comes out clean using the toothpick method.
  9. Remove cake from the oven and leave it to cool. In the meantime, make the frosting.
  10. Cream butter and cream cheese until smooth. Beat in vanilla. Add confectioners sugar and beat on low speed until combined, then on high until frosting is smooth.
  11. Spread frosting on cooled cake.
  12. Serve and enjoy!
